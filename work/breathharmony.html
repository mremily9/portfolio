BREATH HARMONY

DESIGN 301, 302 @ UOA

How might we utilise mindfulness techniques and XR to quickly alleviate mental distress immersively, such as stress and anxiety, for young adults?
I wanted to look at answering this question by creating an immersive AR mobile application that uses AR to guide users to follow the AR animation in its movements, following breathing techniques. This also lets them learn about breathing techniques seen in mindfulness. Breath Harmony also tracks progress and helps motivate the user as they continue to use the application.

Featured on the ReDesign website.

Type: Design

Programs: Miro, Figma, and SnapLens.

Skills: Design thinking, AR, UI/UX prototyping, 3D modelling + animation, and brand design.

Date: Oct 2024

Client: Empathic Computing Institute
