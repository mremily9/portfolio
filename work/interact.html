INTERACT

DESIGN 240 @ UOA

Starting with our choosen statistic of "Every year over 100,000 tonnes of perfectly good food is wasted.", we looked into creating an interactive piece to help the users who interact with it to then help learn how they can make a change in their day to day life in helping make this statstic better. Here we looked towards placing this within a bus stop where people waiting for the bus can interact with it. While they interact with the piece they learn what is considered food and what can be considered as waste, to prevent unnecessary food waste to be thrown out.

Type: Code + Design
Programs: Miro, Processing, Java, and Blender3D.
Skills: Design thinking, Documentation of design process, Java Code + Programming, 3D Modelling, and Sketching.

Date: 2024

Worked in collaboration with Alvin Shum. Supervised by Steffan Hooper in DESIGN 240: Designing with Data @ UOA
