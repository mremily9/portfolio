VENTILUXE

DESIGN 243 @ UOA

Ventiluxe looks into the growing troubles of people who have asthma, developing an assistive technology which is in a form of a mask that filters air and also tracks the users breathing patterns and dosage thorughout the day. With an application that serves as a mthod to link the users data and to help them learn and assist themselves.
Creating a physical prototype of the mask, a digital prototype of the mobile application, and a seprate application that tracks heart rate to display our project.

Type: Product Design
Programs: Cura Ultimaker, Blender, Figma, and Miro.
Skills: Design thinking, Documentation of design process, 3D printing, Graphic Design, Branding, and Prototyping.

Date: 2023

Worked in collaboration with Benjamin Teo, Silvia Yang, and Saxon Zhou.

Supervised by Dr. Roy Davies in DESIGN 243: Design and Assistive Technologies @ UOA

Digital UI/UX prototype made by mainly by Saxon and Silvia.
